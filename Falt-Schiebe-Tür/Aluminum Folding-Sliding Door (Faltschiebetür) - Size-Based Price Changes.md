## 3. Observations on Size-Based Pricing
*   **Width Impact**: Increasing the width has a slightly higher impact on the price than increasing the height, as it often requires more material for the tracks and potentially more folding elements.
*   **Non-Linear Scaling**: The price does not scale linearly with the area. As dimensions approach the maximum limits (3000mm width for 3-part, 6000mm for 6-part), the price per m² increases due to the need for more robust hardware and thicker glass.
*   **Threshold Effects**: Certain size increments may trigger a mandatory upgrade to thicker glass (e.g., from 4mm to 6mm), which adds a significant step-up in the base price.
