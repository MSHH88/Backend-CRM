## Lamelle / Endleiste Colors
By default, the slat color matches the box/rail color. However, they can be configured independently.

| Metallic-Grau | DB 703 | `fs1_06_i` | 0,00 € |

**Note:** Changing the slat color independently of the box color does not seem to add an additional surcharge beyond the initial color surcharge if a non-standard color is already selected for the box.

## Compatibility Rules
- **Standard:** Slat color matches Box/Rail color.
- **Independent Selection:** Users can mix and match any of the 6 available colors for slats vs. box.
- **Aufsatzraffstore:** For the Aufsatz (top-mounted) type, the box is often hidden or integrated, but the same color options apply to the visible rails and slats.
