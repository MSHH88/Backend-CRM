## Calculation Observations
- **Width Impact:** Increasing width from 1000 to 1250 (+25%) increased the price by ~31.7%.
- **Height Impact:** Increasing height from 1000 to 1250 (+25%) at a 2000mm width increased the price by ~5.3%.
- **Non-Linearity:** The price does not increase linearly with area. There are likely "price brackets" or a base manufacturing cost plus material surcharges.
- **Maße Übernehmen:** The price only updates after clicking the "Sonderwünsche übernehmen" button or pressing Enter in the input fields.
