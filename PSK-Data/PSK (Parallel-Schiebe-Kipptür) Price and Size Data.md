# PSK (Parallel-Schiebe-Kipptür) Price and Size Data

This document details the pricing structure and size constraints for PSK doors on fenstermaxx24.com.

## Size Constraints and Technical Limits

The configurator enforces strict technical limits for PSK doors. Sizes outside these ranges are not calculated and result in an error message.

| Material | Manufacturer | Min Width | Max Width | Min Height | Max Height |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aluminium** | Aluprof (MB-70/86) | 1500 mm | 3000 mm | 1700 mm | 2400 mm |
| **Kunststoff** | Aluplast (Neo AD) | 1400 mm | 3000 mm | 800 mm | 2400 mm |
| **Kunststoff** | Gealan (S8000/9000) | 1800 mm | 4100 mm | 1900 mm | 2400 mm |

### Height Pricing Anomaly Resolution (2500mm+)
*   **Verification:** All tested systems (Aluprof, Aluplast, Gealan) have a **hard limit of 2400mm** for height.
*   **Behavior:** Entering 2500mm or higher triggers a "Fehler in der Eingabe" (Input Error) message.
*   **Conclusion:** There is no pricing for heights above 2400mm as they are technically not supported by the standard PSK hardware in this configurator.
