## Hardware and Security Options

The PSK configurator offers several hardware-related surcharges:

1.  **Automatisches Getriebe UP:** Recommended for widths above 2200mm and essential above 2700mm.
2.  **Sicherheitsbeschläge:** RC 1 N (Standard) and RC 2 (WK 2) for enhanced security.
3.  **Reedkontakt:** Maco Tronic sensors for electronic monitoring of the door state.

*Note: Surcharges are typically flat-rate amounts, except for roller shutters and some glass types which may be size-dependent.*
