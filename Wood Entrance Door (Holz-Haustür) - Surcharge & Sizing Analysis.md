## 2. Side Panel Surcharge Size-Dependency

The surcharge for side panels on Drutex wood entrance doors is **highly dependent on the size (width)**. The price increases significantly as the width of the side panel increases. The surcharge is not a fixed amount but is calculated based on the material consumption and complexity of the larger panel.

### Side Panel Surcharge Examples (1-Wing Door, Pine, 1900mm Height):

| Side Panel Width | Initial Price (40% Disc.) | New Price (40% Disc.) | Surcharge (€) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **330 mm** (Min) | € 1,290.46 | € 1,668.65 | **+ € 378.19** |
| **500 mm** | € 1,290.46 | € 1,933.41 | **+ € 642.95** |
| **1000 mm** (Max) | € 1,290.46 | € 2,974.69 | **+ € 1,684.23** |

### Key Findings:

*   **Non-Linear Increase**: The surcharge for the side panel does not increase linearly with width. The price per 100mm of width increases as the panel gets wider.
*   **Material & Labor**: The surcharge reflects the increased material costs (wood, glass, seals) and the additional labor required for a larger, more complex element.
*   **No Fixed Surcharge**: Unlike some other options, the side panel surcharge is not a fixed fee but a dynamic calculation based on the specific dimensions entered.
