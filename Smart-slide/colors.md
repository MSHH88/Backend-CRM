## Color Compatibility
- **Farbe Außen (Exterior Color):**

- **Farbe Innen (Interior Color):**

## General Color Rules
- Colors are automatically selected to be the same for interior and exterior by default.
- Interior white is compatible with all exterior colors.
- Different decorative colors for interior and exterior are available upon request for orders of 10 or more elements.
- Dichtungsfarben (seal colors) are automatically adjusted based on the profile and decorative foil color.
