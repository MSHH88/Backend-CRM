## Calculation Observations
- **Width Impact:** Increasing width from 1550 to 1800 (+16%) increased the price by ~9%.
- **Height Impact:** Increasing height from 800 to 1050 (+31%) at a 2050mm width increased the price by ~13%.
- **Surcharge Scaling:** Note that surcharges for colors and glass also scale with the size of the element. For example, the color surcharge was 233,65 EUR at 1550x800 and increased to 307,65 EUR at 2050x1050.
